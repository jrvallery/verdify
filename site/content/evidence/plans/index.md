# Daily Plans
The lab notebook. Every planning cycle is archived with conditions, decisions, experiments, and a self-assessment score. The planner writes 72-hour setpoint plans three times a day (6 AM, 12 PM, 6 PM Mountain). Each plan carries a hypothesis and an experiment.
The worst day so far was March 25: 88F outdoor, 96.5F indoor peak, 14.2 hours of VPD stress, 203 mister pulses, $1.69 total cost. The planner scored itself a 5 and extracted two lessons. The best recent day was April 1: zero VPD stress hours, $1.15 total cost, score 8.
